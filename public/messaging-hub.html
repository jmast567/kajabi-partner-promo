The Big Picture
This isn't a regular promo month.

50% off Kajabi is already one of the most compelling offers we run. This time, we're layering in three new product launches at the same time. For your audience, this is the best moment to start on Kajabi in a long time. For you, it's one of the biggest earning windows of the year.

Your job is simple: help your audience understand what's possible on Kajabi right now, and make the case for why this month is the right time to start.

The Campaign Frame

Campaign line: "If you don't know X, you don't know Kajabi."

Kajabi has expanded in ways most people don't realize yet. Backstage. Amplify. A smarter Cofounder. These aren't small updates. They change what's possible for experts building businesses on the platform. Use this frame to create a sense of discovery.

"If you don't know Backstage, you don't know what Kajabi can do for coaches."
"If you don't know Amplify, you don't know how Kajabi helps you grow without paid ads."
"If you don't know the new Cofounder, you don't know how much of your business it can run."
Audience Framing by Type
Coaches & consultants

Lead with Backstage. Then the 50% off and Cofounder. Kajabi now handles your full business — digital products, high-ticket delivery, and day-to-day operations.

Course creators

Lead with 50% off and the platform story. Layer in Amplify for growth and Cofounder for efficiency.

Service providers

Lead with Backstage. Stop delivering client work out of a patchwork of tools. Kajabi has a real system for it now.

Beginners / considering starting

Lead with the offer. Keep product details light. Focus on what's possible rather than feature names.

Tone & Voice
Direct.

Say what you mean. Skip the setup.

Confident.

You've used Kajabi. You believe in it. Let that come through.

Human.

Sound like yourself, not a marketing deck.

Outcome-first.

What can your audience build? Start there.

Words & phrases to avoid
Game-changing Revolutionary All-in-one solution Vague income promises Hype language
What This Promo Is Not
Don't frame this as a deal-hunting moment. The audience shouldn't feel like they're getting Kajabi at a discount because Kajabi needs customers. They're getting a rare moment to start on a serious platform at a lower entry cost.

This is the right platform for experts. May is the right time to start because the pricing makes it easy. That's the message.

FTC Disclosure Requirements

Required reading before publishing any promotional content.

As a Kajabi partner, you earn a commission when someone signs up through your affiliate link. The FTC requires you to clearly disclose this relationship whenever you promote Kajabi — in emails, on social media, in videos, in blog posts, anywhere. This isn't optional.

Clear & ConspicuousEasy to see and understand. Buried disclaimers in small text at the bottom don't qualify.
Close to the ClaimPut it near your recommendation — not disconnected at the bottom of the page.
Plain Language"This is an affiliate link" is better than "see disclosure."

Ready-to-use disclosure copy

✉️Email

Place near the top or directly adjacent to your affiliate link.

Heads up: I'm a Kajabi affiliate. If you sign up through my link, I may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.
Quick note: I partner with Kajabi and earn a commission if you sign up through my link.
📸Instagram & Facebook

Must appear before "see more" truncation.

#Ad | #Affiliate | #KajabiPartner
Paid partnership with Kajabi. I earn a commission when you sign up through my link.
𝕏X (Twitter)

Include in the post itself.

#ad or #affiliate
Affiliate link below. I earn a commission if you sign up.
▶️YouTube

Disclose verbally in first 30 seconds AND in description.

This video contains affiliate links. If you purchase through my link, I may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.
⚠️ Important: This is general guidance based on FTC requirements as of 2026. It is not legal advice. For specific compliance questions, consult a legal professional or review the FTC's endorsement guides at ftc.gov.
